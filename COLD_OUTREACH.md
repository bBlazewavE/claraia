# ClaraIA — Cold Outreach Plan

## Target Segments (Brazilian Companies)

### 🏥 1. Clínicas e Consultórios
High volume of WhatsApp messages, appointment scheduling, FAQ. Perfect for chatbot automation.

**How to find:** Google "clínica [cidade]", Instagram search, Google Maps
**Examples to search for in your region:**
- Clínicas odontológicas
- Clínicas de estética
- Consultórios médicos
- Clínicas veterinárias
- Psicólogos e terapeutas

### 🏋️ 2. Academias e Estúdios
Need scheduling, plans/pricing info, class reminders. Most still use WhatsApp manually.

**How to find:** Google Maps, Instagram
**Examples:**
- Academias de musculação
- Estúdios de pilates/yoga
- CrossFit boxes
- Escolas de dança

### 🍕 3. Restaurantes e Delivery
Menu questions, orders, reservations — all automatable.

**How to find:** iFood listings, Google Maps, Instagram
**Examples:**
- Restaurantes com delivery próprio
- Hamburguerias artesanais
- Pizzarias
- Confeitarias e docerias

### 🏠 4. Imobiliárias
High message volume: "quanto custa?", "tem disponível?", filtro por região/preço. Chatbot is a game changer.

**How to find:** OLX, ZAP Imóveis, Google
**Examples:**
- Imobiliárias regionais
- Corretores autônomos com volume

### 🛒 5. E-commerce / Lojas Online
Order tracking, returns, product questions — repetitive support.

**How to find:** Instagram shops, Mercado Livre sellers
**Examples:**
- Lojas de roupas
- Lojas de acessórios/joias
- Pet shops online
- Lojas de suplementos

### 💼 6. Escritórios de Contabilidade
Document processing, client onboarding, FAQ automation.

**How to find:** Google, LinkedIn
**Examples:**
- Contabilidades com 50+ clientes
- Empresas de BPO financeiro

### 🎓 7. Escolas e Cursos
Enrollment questions, schedule info, payment reminders.

**How to find:** Google, Instagram
**Examples:**
- Escolas de idiomas
- Cursos livres / profissionalizantes
- Autoescolas

## How to Find Specific Companies

### Google Maps Method:
1. Go to Google Maps
2. Search: "clínica odontológica [sua cidade]" or "academia [sua cidade]"
3. Look at the ones with good reviews but no chatbot/automation
4. Get their Instagram/email from the listing

### Instagram Method:
1. Search hashtags: #clinicaBH, #restauranteBH, #academiaBH (swap city)
2. Look at business profiles with 1K-50K followers
3. Check if they respond to DMs manually (most do)
4. DM them

### LinkedIn Method:
1. Search: "proprietário restaurante Minas Gerais" or "gerente clínica"
2. Connect + message
